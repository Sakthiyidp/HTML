Tolkappiyam

Tolkappiyam is an ancient extant Tamil grammar text considered to be the oldest surviving work of Tamil literature.

Poetry Books

Books in the poetry genre contain words that follow a rhythm or structure, and sometimes rhyme, that are designed on evoke emotion and thought. Books can be, but aren't often written entirely in poetic form.

Biogrpahy Books

A biography is simply the story of a real person's life. It could be about a person who is still alive, someone who lived centuries ago, someone who is globally famous, an unsung hero forgotten by history, or even a unique group of people.
